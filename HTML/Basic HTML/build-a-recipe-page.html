Chocolate Chip Cookies

This recipe will guide you through making delicious homemade chocolate chip cookies!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of butter
  • 1 cup of white sugar
  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup of chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Cream together the butter and sugar in a bowl.
  3. Add the flour gradually and mix well.
  4. Fold in the chocolate chips and scoop onto a baking sheet.
